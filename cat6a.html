Category 6 Augmented (Cat6a)

The professional choice for heavy data and future-proofing.

Faster data travels through Cat6a at 10 Gbps across every meter of its hundred-meter reach. Better protection wraps the wires inside, allowing it to meet resistance from electromagnetic interference because of its 500 MHz frequency.

Best Suited For:

  • Enterprise office networks
  • Power over Ethernet (PoE) applications
  • Networks designed for future upgrades

Quick Specs

Speed: 10 Gbps (full 100m)

Frequency: 500 MHz

Build: Durable / Heavily Shielded
