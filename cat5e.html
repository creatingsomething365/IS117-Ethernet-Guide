Category 5e (Cat5e)

The reliable standard for everyday home networking.

As the successor to Cat5, Cat5e cables handle one gigabit per second across runs as long as 100 meters. Usually seen in homes or compact offices, these wires deliver solid performance without high costs.

Best Suited For:

  • Everyday internet use and web browsing
  • High-definition streaming
  • Small-scale office networks

Quick Specs

Speed: 1 Gbps

Max Distance: 100 Meters

Common Use: Residential
