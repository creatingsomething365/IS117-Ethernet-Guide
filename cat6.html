Category 6 (Cat6)

Higher performance for busy networks and gaming.

Even though Cat6 cables can handle 10 Gbps, that speed is limited to 55 meters. Beyond that distance, they maintain 1 Gbps all the way to 100 meters. Twisting the wires tighter inside reduces crosstalk between them.

Best Suited For:

  • Online gaming and low-latency needs
  • High-speed internet subscriptions
  • Mid-sized office networks

Quick Specs

Speed: 10 Gbps (up to 55m)

Frequency: 250 MHz

Feature: Reduced Crosstalk
